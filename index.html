DON'T LET PEST TAKE OVER YOUR HOME.

Mice

Mice can at times be harmful. Damaging and eating food causing structural damage and spreading diseases through their parasites and feces. During and after the treatment, we look for opening where mice can enter. So, they can be sealed to prevent new mice from enterning.

Cockroach

Cockroach can be carriers of various diseases because they are commonly found near waste deposits or in the kitchen, where food is present. Cockroaches may be found hiding beneath rubber mats, behind wallpaper and ithin wall cracks. We control the entry and reproduction of cockroach.

Termites

Termites cause various damage. They primarily feed on wood but also damage paper, books, and even swimming pool liners and filtration systems. They can also injure living trees and shrubs. They are often seen around windows and doors. Termite-damaged wood is hollowed out along the grain. We prevent the termites from entering and infecting buildings.
